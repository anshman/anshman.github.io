    FitInfinite is Breaking down the barriers to fitness

    FitInfinite is for anyone juggling multiple priorities while on the move; for those who don’t want to exercise alone, those who don’t have the time, those with safety concerns, or those with any other excuses.

our vision

At FitInfinite we're passionate about both community and fitness.

We believe that community is central to motivation and leading a healthy, active life.

The human costs of inactivity and ill health are immense. Bringing communities together and promoting physical activity in safe, welcoming environments is our solution to combating this.

Our goal is to break down the barriers to participation in any sports or fitness activity.

We believe that everyone should be able to effortlessly find and engage in a fitness activity that they enjoy with someone they can trust.

how fitinfinite works

  • realtime

    FitInfinite helps you discover the fitness activities that you enjoy. And it does this in realtime. So whether you're looking for a sports buddy in the next 30 minutes, a fitness professional tomorrow evening or a Saturday afternoon group session FitInfinite will help you find them.

  • location aware

    FitInfinite uses your mobile phone to show you the best fitness activities close to you. We don't track you or follow you. Being location aware Fitnfinite will always help you find a fun, convenient fitness experience wherever you are.

  • mobile

    With FitInfinite you manage everything on your smartphone. Easily find fitness activities, book fitness professionals, pay for sessions  – all with a few taps. You also get notifications on upcoming activities and can message sports buddies. FitInfinite is your on-the-go fitness management solution.

  • community focus

    FitInfinite's goal is to be an open, transparent fitness marketplace. Everything we've developed has been in close contact with our community. We've added ratings features, discovery features, messaging features and more based on the excellent feedback we've gotten so far. This is also how we plan to keep developing FitInfinite.
